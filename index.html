About Us

Mitt Arv, is an online platform that understands the important of expressing emotion and feelings, especially during difficult times. We believe that everyone deserves a safe space where they can share their innermost thoughts and connect with their loved ones.


We understand that talking about sensitive topics can be challenging, and that is why we have created a platform that feels like a friend. Our goal is to provide a space where you can be vulnerable and authentic without fear of judgement of shame.
